Poolish

Let`s get started!

In this recipe we are going to prepare a poolish for the pizza dough

Poolish is a prefermented flour and water mixture, it is easier to handle than a pizza dough since it will be smaller and it reduces the proofing time greatly

Poolish needs to be prepared 16-24 hours before the desired time of pizza preparation

ingredients

  • water
  • flour
  • honey
  • yeast

Instructions

  1. Simply mix water (300ml), flour (300g), honey (5g) and yeast (5g) together and mix until it becomes homogenious.

When everything is mixed together put it in a closed container and let it rest in room temperature for an hour, after an hour rest put it in the refrigrator and let it rest in the fridge at least 16 hours but no more than 24 hours.
