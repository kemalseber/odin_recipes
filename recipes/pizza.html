Pizza dough

Let`s get started!

This is a recipe for the perfect pizza dough, we are going to use the poolish that we prepared beforehand for this recipe

ingredients

  • poolish
  • water
  • flour
  • Salt
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. Pour the water in a bowl and pour the poolish inside, mix it until the mixture becomes homogenous
  2. Add the flour, salt and olive oil and kneed in the bowl
  3. Sprinkle some flour on your table and continue kneeding on the table for 10-12 minutes. You can use pulling and folding method for kneeding the dough.
  4. Let the dough rest until it doubles in size in a closed container
  5. Divide the dough to 250g pieces and make a ball shape. Let them rest until they double in size in a closed container.
  6. Simple apply pressure with your finger tips to the middle of the ball and strech it to a pizza shape.

You can put your sauce and toppings and bake it in the oven now (always use the highest setting of the oven and preheat).

If you are using a conventional oven let the base bake for a few minutes with the sauce before adding the other toppings.
